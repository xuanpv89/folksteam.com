Use this checklist before running a Compassio-style session with La Ban Cam Xuc, a podcast prompt, an article, or a live workshop topic.

## Session Preparation

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Clarify the purpose">
    - What is the session for: check-in, learning, relationship repair, career reflection, or creative expression?
    - What should people leave with: awareness, language, one action, or a next support step?
  </Card>
  <Card title="Choose the material">
    - Pick one Compassio theme or one Emotional Compass card set.
    - Avoid mixing too many topics in one session.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Set the container">
    - Make sharing optional.
    - Keep advice-giving limited.
    - Remind participants that emotional awareness is not clinical diagnosis.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## Session Run Sheet

<Steps>
  1. **Opening:** Ask each person to choose one word or one card for their current state.
  2. **Prompt:** Introduce one Compassio content piece or one Emotional Compass card.
  3. **Private reflection:** Give people quiet time to write before asking anyone to speak.
  4. **Pair or group sharing:** Use active listening: reflect first, advise only if invited.
  5. **Synthesis:** Name patterns without exposing anyone's private details.
  6. **Close:** Each person chooses one next action, boundary, request, or question.
</Steps>

## Facilitator Checklist

- Prepare cards, writing materials, and one clear prompt.
- Decide how long each sharing round will be.
- Keep the tone warm, specific, and non-performative.
- Watch for people who need a pause or a gentler option.
- Offer booking or professional support when the topic needs deeper care.
