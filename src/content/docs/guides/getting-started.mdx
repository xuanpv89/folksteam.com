## First Practice Flow

<Steps>
  1. **Pick one theme.** Choose mental healthcare, career, love, self development, emotion, or perspective.
  2. **Choose one source.** Use a Compassio article, podcast episode, video, event topic, or Emotional Compass card.
  3. **Create a safe container.** Make participation optional and set a boundary for sharing.
  4. **Name the signal.** Ask: What am I feeling? What triggered it? What might this feeling be asking for?
  5. **Choose one action.** End with a small action that can be done within a week.
</Steps>

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Active Listening" icon="seti:yaml">

    Use Relation Ship when the goal is to separate love from attachment, dependency, intimacy, passion, and commitment.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Happy Alone" icon="star">
    Use Doc Cung Compassio when reflecting on solitude, loneliness, self-connection, and chosen alone time.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Aside type="tip" title="Keep it gentle">
  Compassio practices work best when they are invitational. Do not force disclosure, diagnose people, or turn reflection into a performance.
</Aside>
