Compassio programs help people learn and practice emotional literacy through content, events, reflection tools, publications, and expert sessions.

The original Compassio ecosystem includes themes such as Podcast & Video, Mental Healthcare, Career, Love, Self Development, Emotion, Perspective, Shop, Events, Experts, Publications, and Content Collaboration.

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Mental Healthcare" icon="star">
    Practical reflection content for slowing down, naming inner signals, and building self-support habits.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Career" icon="rocket">
    Conversations about motivation, career choices, workplace relationships, and the emotional side of professional life.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Love and Relationship" icon="star">
    Relationship literacy for understanding intimacy, passion, commitment, attachment, dependency, and communication.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Emotion" icon="pencil">
    Emotional Compass practices that help people name feelings, understand messages, and choose aligned responses.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
