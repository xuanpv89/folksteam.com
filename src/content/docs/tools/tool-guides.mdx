Compassio is organized around content and practices for mental healthcare, career, love, self development, emotion, personal perspective, podcast/video, events, expert booking, publications, and content collaboration.

Use this page as a guide library. Each guide turns a Compassio topic into a concrete practice that can be used by individuals, facilitators, teachers, coaches, teams, or community builders.

## Guide Library

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Emotional Check-in Guide" icon="star">
    Use La Ban Cam Xuc to help individuals or groups name their current emotional state without forcing over-sharing.

    - Choose one card or one feeling word.
    - Name the signal, need, and possible message.
    - Share one sentence only if the person feels ready.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Active Listening Guide" icon="seti:yaml">
    Based on Compassio's Hoc Nhanh, Song Cham active listening content.

    - Listen without preparing a counterargument.
    - Reflect back what you heard before giving advice.
    - Ask one clarifying question before moving to solutions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Career Reflection Guide" icon="rocket">
    Based on Cai Nghe Cai Nghiep and the emotional side of choosing work.

    - Identify the pressure, desire, fear, and value behind a career decision.
    - Separate external expectation from personal direction.
    - Choose one experiment instead of demanding one final answer.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Relationship Literacy Guide" icon="seti:yaml">
    Based on Relation Ship and Compassio's love/relationship content.

    - Clarify whether the situation involves intimacy, passion, commitment, attachment, or dependency.
    - Name the need before judging the other person.
    - Turn vague hurt into a specific request.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Happy Alone Guide" icon="star">
    Based on Doc Cung Compassio's reflection on being alone and being lonely.

    - Ask whether the alone time is chosen or forced.
    - Use solitude for recovery, sorting, and self-connection.
    - Notice when isolation needs support from another person.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Creative Reflection Guide" icon="pencil">
    Based on Compassio's creative workshop direction.

    - Turn one feeling into a line, image, lyric, or color.
    - Share the creative output only after the person has named what it means.
    - End with one action the creative insight points toward.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## Recommended Workflow

<Steps>
  1. **Pick the context.** Is this for self-reflection, a classroom, a team check-in, a coaching session, or a workshop?
  2. **Choose one Compassio theme.** Mental healthcare, career, love, self development, emotion, or perspective.
  3. **Select one prompt.** Use a card, podcast topic, article, or short practice.
  4. **Create a safe container.** Make participation optional, keep sharing bounded, and avoid diagnosing people.
  5. **Move from feeling to action.** End with one small next step, not a dramatic life decision.
</Steps>

## Practice Formats

### 5-minute check-in

- Each person chooses one emotion card or feeling word.
- Each person says: "I am noticing ___, and what I need from the group is ___."
- No one gives advice unless the person asks for it.

### 20-minute group reflection

- Start with one Compassio podcast/article topic.
- Ask people to write privately for 5 minutes.
- Pair-share using active listening.
- Close with one action or one boundary.

### 60-minute workshop

- Open with La Ban Cam Xuc.
- Introduce one theme: work, love, listening, solitude, emotion, or self development.
- Use journaling, pair sharing, and small-group synthesis.
- End with a clear personal or group commitment.
